Flicksy - FAQ
What is Flicksy? ▶
Flicksy is a futuristic movie and series discovery platform that curates personalized recommendations for you.
How do I create an account? ▶
Simply click on the 'Create Profile' button on the homepage and follow the prompts to set up your account.
Is Flicksy free to use? ▶
Yes! Flicksy is completely free to use, with optional premium features coming soon.
Can I save movies to watch later? ▶
Absolutely! Use the "Add to Watchlist" button on any movie or series detail page.
How does Flicksy recommend movies? ▶
Flicksy provides recommendations based on collaborative filtering, analyzing what movies and shows people with similar tastes have watched and enjoyed. Our system also considers a title's popularity and overall ratings.
